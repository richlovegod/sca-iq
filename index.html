    ◎ 名詞速查：業許可 vs 承認◎ Quick Glossary: Business Licence vs Marketing Approval

    MAH（製造販賣業者）＝法律上掛名賣藥、負全責的主體（品質 GQP＋安全 GVP）。此處＝REPROCELL。MAH (marketing authorisation holder) = the entity legally named as the seller and bearing full responsibility (quality GQP + safety GVP). Here that is REPROCELL.

    業許可（製造販賣業許可）＝公司「能賣藥的營業執照」。神奈川縣核發，審查約需 3 個月（最慢 2026 Q3 可望核准）。Business licence (manufacturing/marketing business licence) = the company's licence to sell drugs at all. Issued by Kanagawa Prefecture; review takes about 3 months (approval expected by Q3 2026 at the latest).

    承認（製造販賣承認＝藥證）＝這支藥「本身」獲准。PMDA 審查 → MHLW 核發。Marketing approval (the product licence itself) = approval of this particular drug. Reviewed by PMDA, issued by MHLW.

    PMDA＝日本藥品審查機關（≈ 美國 FDA 審查部門）。做產品安全/療效科學審查。PMDA = Japan's drug review agency (roughly the review arm of the US FDA). Conducts the scientific safety/efficacy review.

    → 兩者平行、都要有才能開賣。分工：仲恩(台灣)製造，REPROCELL 負責日本銷售。→ The two run in parallel and both are required before sales can start. Division of labour: Steminent manufactures in Taiwan, REPROCELL sells in Japan.
